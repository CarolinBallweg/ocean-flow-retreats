The Experience

Connect with the ocean and with yourself in a beautiful environment and treat yourself. 2 nights in a luxurious hotel, 4 nights on a sailboat in a group of 5–6 people (plus crew and organizers).



What awaits you

09–11 June 2025 – Arrival & Preparation

Steigenberger Coraya Beach Hotel, Marsa Alam

  • Land gently, rest, and meet the group
  • Learn the basics of freediving, water dance & body awareness in water
  • Feel supported as you ease into the ocean and into connection

11–15 June 2025 – Life on the Sea

4 nights on a sail boat – on board of a Beneteau Cyclades 50.5

  • Sailing through marine sanctuaries & coral gardens
  • Swimming with wild dolphins
  • Yoga and meditation on deck
  • Snorkeling, breathwork & somatic sessions in water
  • Shared meals, shared presence, shared space

We’ll explore

  • Sataya Dolphin House – one of the world’s best places to meet dolphins in the wild
  • Hamata Island & Abo Galawa Reef – vibrant reefs and a sunken wreck

Meet your facilitators

Carolin Ballweg

Carolin brings grounded presence and a deep connection to the ocean. She creates space for others to soften, explore, and reconnect — with breath, water, and self. She’s a freediving athlete, freediving instructor, breathwork & ice bath guide, and former management consultant. Originally from Germany, she now lives and trains in Dahab.

Anne Wuchold

Anne leads with intuition and embodiment. Her work invites softness, depth, and presence — using water as a space for healing and reconnection. She’s an experienced retreat facilitator, freediving instructor, water dance & water therapy practitioner, and massage therapist — also based in Dahab, originally from Germany.

Together we create a safe, open space for real connection — with nature, the body, and yourself.
